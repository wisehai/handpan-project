WisePan 隐私政策

最后更新：2026年7月8日

WisePan 是一款离线应用。 本应用不需要用户注册或登录，不连接互联网，不收集、存储、上传或分享任何个人信息。

1. 我们收集的信息

WisePan 不收集任何个人信息。

本应用不会收集以下信息：

  • 姓名、邮箱、电话号码等身份信息
  • 账号信息或登录信息
  • 位置信息
  • 通讯录、照片、文件或其他个人内容
  • 设备标识符、广告标识符或分析数据
  • 使用行为数据或崩溃分析数据

2. 网络连接

WisePan 是离线应用，正常使用过程中不需要连接互联网。

本应用不会将任何数据上传到服务器，也不会从服务器下载与用户相关的数据。

3. 用户登录

WisePan 不提供账号注册或登录功能。

用户无需创建账号即可使用本应用。

4. 第三方服务

WisePan 不集成第三方统计、广告、登录、支付或追踪服务。

本应用不会向第三方共享用户数据。

5. 权限使用

WisePan 不主动请求与个人隐私相关的系统权限，例如定位、通讯录、相机、麦克风或照片访问权限。

如果未来版本需要使用某些系统权限，我们会在应用中说明用途，并更新本隐私政策。

6. 儿童隐私

WisePan 不会有意收集任何儿童的个人信息。由于本应用不收集任何个人信息，因此也不会收集儿童的个人信息。

7. 数据安全

由于 WisePan 不收集、存储或上传个人信息，因此不存在由本应用维护的个人数据服务器或用户数据库。

8. 隐私政策的变更

如果本隐私政策发生变化，我们会在本页面更新最新内容，并修改“最后更新”日期。

9. 联系我们

WisePan Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

WisePan is an offline app. The app does not require registration or login, does not connect to the Internet, and does not collect, store, upload, or share any personal information.

1. Information We Collect

WisePan does not collect any personal information.

The app does not collect:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or other identity information
  • Account or login information
  • Location information
  • Contacts, photos, files, or other personal content
  • Device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or analytics data
  • Usage behavior data or crash analytics data

2. Network Connection

WisePan is an offline app and does not require an Internet connection for normal use.

The app does not upload any data to servers and does not download user-related data from servers.

3. User Login

WisePan does not provide account registration or login features.

Users can use the app without creating an account.

4. Third-Party Services

WisePan does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, login, payment, or tracking services.

The app does not share user data with third parties.

5. Permissions

WisePan does not actively request system permissions related to personal privacy, such as location, contacts, camera, microphone, or photo library access.

If a future version requires certain system permissions, we will explain the purpose in the app and update this Privacy Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

WisePan does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since the app does not collect any personal information, it does not collect personal information from children either.

7. Data Security

Since WisePan does not collect, store, or upload personal information, there are no personal data servers or user databases maintained by this app.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, we will update the latest version on this page and revise the “Last updated” date.
